Here are some fun and quirky facts about Buddhism you probably didn’t know!

  1. Buddha isn’t a god — he was a human Siddhartha Gautama was a prince who achieved enlightenment through meditation, not divine powers.
  2. Unlike religions that have fixed rules or commandments, Buddhism teaches ethical guidelines
    • Don’t harm living beings
    • Don’t steal
    • Avoid sexual misconduct
    • Don’t lie
    • Avoid intoxicants (like drugs or alcohol)
  3. Buddhism doesn’t worship in one specific place You can meditate anywhere — a temple, a forest, a mountain, or even at home.
  4. Buddhist monks eat only what people offer them In many countries, monks walk around every morning with bowls, collecting food from locals.
  5. There are thousands of styles of meditation From breathing meditation to walking meditation even laughing meditation exists.
