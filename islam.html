Here are some fascinating facts about Islam a faith rooted in Salām (peace) and Īmān (belief).

  1. Second largest and fastest growing religion by birth and conversion.
  2. Its holy book is The Quran known for its 100% consistency and preservation (due to oral transmission)
  3. Five pillars of islam :-
    • Shahādah (Declaration of Faith)
    • Ṣalāh (Prayer)
    • Zakāh (Charity)
    • Ṣawm (Fasting in Ramadan))
    • Ḥajj (Pilgrimage to Makkah)
  4. From Adam, Nuh (Noah), Ibrahim (Abraham), Musa (Moses), Isa (Jesus) Islam sees them all as prophets of Allah.
  5. Muslims face the Kaaba only for direction (Qiblah) during prayer they worship Allah alone, not the structure.
